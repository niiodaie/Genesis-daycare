About Genesis Royalty

Introducing a warm, nurturing place to learn and grow. We build strong foundations through safe relationships in a safe environment, so that every child may have success in school and beyond.

Our Story

GENESIS Royalty Daycare Center LLC is a Limited Liability Company founded by Mariam Marie Jalloh, dedicated to providing high-quality, faith-aligned childcare in Purcellville, Virginia.

Operating in partnership with a local church facility at 37018 Glendale St., we focus on early childhood learning, character development, and enrichment activities to support both children and working families in the Purcellville area.

Our mission is to deliver high-quality, faith-aligned care that encourages social, emotional, and cognitive growth while giving parents peace of mind.

Our Approach

Educational Philosophy

At Genesis Royalty Daycare Center, we believe a solid foundation for learning comes from collaborative relationships with responsive and respectful teams. Our approach is tailored to support little ones in growing, both academically and personally.

Beginning with infants, we work each day to prepare your little one for success in elementary school and beyond. Our research-based instruction plan and individualized learning approach are the basis for every program.

Faith-Aligned Values

Our partnership with a local church facility allows us to integrate faith-based values into our daily activities, promoting character development, compassion, and moral growth alongside academic learning.

We create an environment where children learn about kindness, respect, and community while developing their cognitive and social skills.

Health and Safety

At Genesis Royalty, we promote health and safety awareness through:

  • Teaching and encouraging regular handwashing (before meals, after restroom use, and after playing)
  • Promoting covering coughs and sneezes with a tissue or elbow
  • Maintaining clean and sanitized environments
  • Following all state health and safety regulations
  • Providing nutritious meals and snacks following USDA guidelines

Features Families Love

  • Nutritious meals and snacks (USDA guidelines)
  • Indoor & outdoor play spaces
  • Daily parent reports & communication
  • Developmental & faith-based activities
  • Flexible full-time and part-time schedules
  • After-school care programs
  • Primary caregiver system for infants
